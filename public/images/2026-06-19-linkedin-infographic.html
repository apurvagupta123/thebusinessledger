What the money flow tells us

Foreign institutional investors sold ₹1,025 Cr — repricing Indian IT against an AI-disrupted landscape. But domestic institutions bought ₹3,517 Cr, absorbing the sell-off and preventing a broader crash. DIIs see value; FIIs see risk. The real question: if AI reduces outsourcing demand, does the old India IT story still hold?

The Divergence Explained

US tech is hardware & AI infrastructure — chips, compute, data centres. India IT is services & outsourcing — writing code for US and European clients. If AI can write code faster and cheaper, the market is asking: how much outsourcing survives? Today's session was the market's first loud answer.
